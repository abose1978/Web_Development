    Being Multi-Disciplinary in nature, Instrumentation Engineering is the most happening field of engineering in the world now & the primary goal of Instrumentation Engineering is Automation. The art and science of measurement and control of these process parameters through proper designing and configuring of automated systems in electrical, pneumatic and mechanical domains within a production or manufacturing area is known as Instrumentation Engineering.

    Instrumentation Engineering majorly contributes in improving the productivity, optimisation, stability, reliability, safety and continuity of industries. The key devices/Instruments & technologies used to attain these are Sensors Transducers Transmitters, Valves, Actuators, Solenoids, Positioners, Internet of Things (IoT), Artificial Intellegince (AI), Machine Learning (ML) Controllers, Microprocessors & Microcontrollers, Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC), Robotics, Distributed Control System (DCS), Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) and Data Science.
